This line chart displays the annual heating and cooling degree days in the contiguous 48 United States from 1895 to 2023. It illustrates two opposing long-term trends over more than a century. The number of heating degree days, which represent colder conditions, shows a general decline, particularly in recent decades. Conversely, the number of cooling degree days, representing warmer conditions, exhibits a clear upward trend, indicating a rising need for cooling.
